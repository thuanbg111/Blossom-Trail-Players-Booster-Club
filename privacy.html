Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Blossom Trail Players Booster Club (“we”, “our”) handles information on this website. We design our site to be simple and privacy‑friendly, especially on mobile devices.

Summary: We only collect information you provide in the contact form and basic technical logs to keep the site secure. We do not sell your data or use third‑party advertising trackers.

Last updated: February 19, 2025

Scope

This policy covers this website and any pages linked under the btp booster club domain. It does not cover third‑party sites we link to (e.g., Google Maps).

Information we collect

  • Contact form — your name, email, and message content when you submit the form.
  • Operational logs — automatically captured limited technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type, date/time) to operate and secure the site.

How we use information

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support to students, families, and community members.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website, including preventing abuse and troubleshooting.
  • Comply with applicable laws for nonprofit record‑keeping.

Cookies & tracking

We do not use third‑party analytics or advertising cookies. Essential cookies (if any) are only used to keep the site working and are not used to track you across sites.

Sharing & disclosure

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We may share information with service providers (e.g., website hosting) strictly to run this site, under confidentiality obligations.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

Data retention

We keep contact form messages only as long as needed to respond and for basic nonprofit records, then delete or anonymize them.

Your choices & rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of information you have submitted via the contact form.
  • You can opt not to submit the contact form and instead email us directly.

To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

Our programs serve students, but this website is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children online without consent from a parent or guardian when required by law.

International visitors

Our organization and website infrastructure are based in the United States. If you access the site from other regions, your information may be processed in the U.S. according to this policy.

Third‑party links

Links to external sites (for example, Google Maps) are provided for convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
