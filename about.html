About Blossom Trail Players Booster Club

Blossom Trail Players Booster Club is a California Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation in Fresno, CA. We support theatrical arts education and performance opportunities for students and the community.

Mission

  • Stage theatrical performances;
  • Provide encouragement and support in the theatrical arts;
  • Offer educational workshops and opportunities.

Public Benefit

Organized exclusively for charitable and educational purposes within the meaning of Internal Revenue Code §501(c)(3).

Governance & Compliance

  • No private inurement to directors, officers, or members;
  • No political campaign activity or substantial lobbying;
  • Upon dissolution, assets go to another §501(c)(3) organization.
