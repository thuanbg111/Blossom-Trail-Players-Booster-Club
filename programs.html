Community Stage

Seasonal theatrical productions open to students and community participants. Roles include acting, directing, stage crew, and technical support.

Workshops & Clinics

Hands-on workshops covering acting fundamentals, voice, movement, lighting, set design, and production management.

Student Support

Mentorship and access to resources that help students gain confidence and experience in the performing arts.
